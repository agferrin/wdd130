Overview

Purpose

Tarqui Ward - Manta Stake will be holding a special event to share with the public information about the beliefs, doctrines, rites and traditions of the members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Manta, Ecuador.

Audience

Members of the Church will invite their friends and family, missionaries will invite their investigators, and an invitation will be shared through local press and social media. This web site will be shared along for people to be informed of all the details of the event.

Content

Home

Have you met a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints?

Have you heard terms like “The Plan of Salvations”, “Sealings”, “The Book of Mormon”, “Family History”? Come and find out what those exactly mean.

Enjoy the voices of the Choir sing Nearer, My God, to Thee. Check the history of our city, its beginnings and projects for the future. Get to know more about us with live action performances. Grab a snack

Agenda

- Opening - 3:00 pm: o History of Manta o Family Search

- The plan of Salvation - 4:00pm: o Premortal life o Mortal life o Resurrection and grades of glory o Temples o The Atonement

- Closure - 4:30pm:

- Extra: o Create you FamilySearch account o Indexing and Records preservation
